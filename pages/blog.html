Some tips for Junior Devs

You can't know everything

You are Junior. Your ride has just begun. Do you feel like you have a lot to learn? Do you feel bad about that? Give up that feeling. Don't you dare to compare yourself with anyone. You're learning, you're building a great developer in you and that will take time.

Be patience

Do you remember that Guns N' Roses song? Yeah, a really wonderful one. Talking seriously, are you trying to tell me that you just started and you're already worried about when you'll be a senior? You must be like a sponge and absorb. Now, I'm asking you. What happens when you squeeze a sponge? Weel, it loses what it absorbs. With this I'm trying to tell you not to rush. Grow up at your own pace.

Your opinion matters

It happens to all of us. We are afraid of sharing our opinion since we think we are going to say something stupid. But, even if you say it, you will learn from it. This is a collaborative work and the best way to learn is to share our lines of thought with the others and to discuss them.
